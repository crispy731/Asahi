asahi

japanese steakhouse

Asahi is a Japanese Steakhouse located in Jackson, Tennessee in The Columns. When you walk in, you will begreeted by the host or hostess that will tell you where to sit. You will have to make a choice between two things. Asahi features two sides for you to choose from. On the left, we feature a habachi style side where you can watch a cook prepare your food in front of you. Our cooks are trained to cook the food to your liking as well as making it an enjoyable experience. On on right, we have a sit down area with a bar to drink at. This side is much more laid back with you sitting at normal tables instead of at the grill. Our menu boasts lots of different options to choose from with the option of drinks from the bar aswell.
