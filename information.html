asahi

japanese steakhouse

Asahi is a Japanese Steakhouse located in Jackson, Tennessee in The Columns. Asahi features two sides for you to choose from. On the left, we feature a habachi style side where you can watch a cook prepare your food in front of your. Our cooks are trained to cook the food to your liking as well as making it an enjoyable experience. On on right, we have a sit down area with a bar to drink at. This side is much more laid back with you sitting at normal tables instead of at the grill. Our menu boasts lots of different options to choose from with drinks from the bar.
